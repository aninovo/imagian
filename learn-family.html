FAMILY



Mother, father: parents.

Parents are represented by silhouettes with babies in hands. The gender is marked with a symbol above.

Two pluses signify plurality.



Grandparents: grandfather, grandmother.

These are compounds with the meaning of "parent of a parent".



Siblings: sister, brother.

The SIBLING symbol is a pair of kids under the same roof, symbolyzing "family".



Family, friends.

FAMILY is represented by people under a roof. FRIEND is represented by one buddy shoulder-hugging another.
